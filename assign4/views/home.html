Introduction to the Website

This website is an educational platform that provides users with a wealth of information on various topics related to education. The site comprises of 3 main sections: Home, About, and HTML Demo. In addition, it features 5 routes that supply data, including All Students, Students by Course, A Single Student By Student Number, Teaching Assistants, and All Courses. These sections are designed to provide you with all the information you need to learn and succeed in your academic pursuits.




The Power of HTML5

This site utilizes HTML5, a markup language used for structuring and presenting content on the World Wide Web. HTML5 provides a wide range of features and technologies that make it an ideal choice for building modern websites, including support for multimedia, enhanced semantic markup, and improved scripting capabilities. Whether you're a seasoned web developer or just starting out, HTML5 is a powerful tool for creating engaging and effective websites
